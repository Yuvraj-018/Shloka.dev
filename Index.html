About Us

Our Mission

Tatsam - The Hindi Society of NSUT is dedicated to preserving and promoting Hindi language and literature in the academic environment of Netaji Subhas University of Technology. We strive to create a platform for students to express themselves creatively in Hindi and explore the rich cultural heritage of India.

Our Vision

To establish Hindi as a vibrant medium of intellectual and creative expression among the student community, bridging the gap between traditional literature and contemporary thought.

Activities

We organize poetry recitations, debates, workshops, literary competitions, and cultural events throughout the academic year, providing opportunities for students to showcase their talents in Hindi.
